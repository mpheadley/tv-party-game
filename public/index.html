Hot Take 🔥

The party game where the worst answer wins.

or join an existing room

How to Play

1
Create a room and share the 4-letter code with your group
2
Everyone joins on their phone — no app needed
3
Answer the prompt — be funny, be weird, be bold
4
Vote for your favorite — the best (worst?) answer wins the round

Two Ways to Play

📺

TV Mode

Cast a laptop to your TV for the full Jackbox-style experience. Prompts and results on the big screen, everyone plays on their phones.

📱

Phone-Only Mode

No TV? No problem. Everyone plays on their own phone. The host's phone shows the big reveal — gather around for the results.

3–16 players • Ages 9+ • Free • No app download
